Our Products

As a premium manufacturer of power-tools and pillar-drilling machines, DOSCHE has been the market leader in Germany since 1965. Thanks to the exclusive use of high quality materials and innovative working methods, the family business DOSCHE has been able to maintain its position in the market for decades and is excellently prepared for the future.

The production site in Stuttgart is divided into two main product divisions. On one hand, power tools, which can be used optimally in private and industrial environments. On the other hand, the industrial pillar-drilling machines, which are mainly used in factories or production lines for precision drilling.

Power-Tools
These machines are now standard equipment not only for DIY enthusiasts. Especially the cordless drill helicopter version is becoming more and more popular and has long since caught up with the normal hand drill.
